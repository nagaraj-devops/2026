1. IP Address

   a. An IP (Internet Protocol) Address is like a mailing address for a computer. Just as a house needs an address so the post office knows where to deliver mail, every device on a network needs an IP address so data knows where to go.
2. VPC (Virtual Private Cloud)

   a. A VPC is your own private floor in a giant skyscraper (the Cloud). Even though other companies are in the same building, your floor is walled off. You control who enters, who leaves, and how the rooms are laid out.
3. Subnet

   A Subnet is a specific room inside your private office floor. You might have one room for "Public" visitors (like a reception desk) and another "Private" room for your safe (sensitive data) that no one from the outside can see.
