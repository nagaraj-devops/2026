# AWS

## Why Cloud?

Think of the cloud like renting an apartment instead of building a house.

- No big upfront costs: You don't have to buy expensive physical servers. You just pay a monthly "rent" for what you use.

- It grows with you: If you suddenly get millions of visitors, you can "rent" more space instantly. When they leave, you stop paying for it.

- Someone else does the chores: You don't have to worry about fixing broken hardware, cooling systems, or electricity. The provider handles the "building," and you just focus on your work.

- Always on: Because your data is stored in multiple professional data centers, your website is much less likely to crash or go offline.
